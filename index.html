Ensnapifacation

Ensnapifacation is a playful card game full of surprises. Players take turns drawing challenge cards and must react quickly — bluffing and laughter encouraged.

How to play (short): each round, one player reads a prompt, others respond with a card. The reader picks the funniest or best match. Rounds are quick and perfect for parties.

Qazacatism

Qazacatism is a quirky family-friendly card game created to spark conversation and silly debates. Expect strange scenarios, creative solutions, and lots of laughs.

Quick overview: Players draw scenario cards and secretly choose a response. Votes are cast and the highest-voted answers win points. Simple setup, big replayability.
